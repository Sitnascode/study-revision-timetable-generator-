Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does the AI generate my timetable?

A: Our AI analyzes your study preferences, subject difficulty, available time, and energy levels to create an optimized schedule that maximizes learning while preventing burnout.

Q: Can I modify the generated timetable?

A: Yes! You can adjust study blocks, change topics, and regenerate the timetable anytime.

Q: Is my data saved?

A: Yes, all your data is saved locally in your browser for privacy and quick access.

Q: Can I export my timetable?

A: Yes, you can export your timetable in PDF, Excel, or text format.

Q: How does the app prevent burnout?

A: The app schedules regular breaks, respects your sleep hours, and distributes study load evenly to prevent exhaustion.
